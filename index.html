What We Stand For

Our Beliefs

Not mission statements. These are the operating principles that govern every project we take on.

01

Outcomes over outputs

We don't measure success by lines of code shipped or slides presented. We measure it in efficiency gained, revenue unlocked, and decisions accelerated. If it doesn't move the needle, we didn't do our job.
02

Diagnosis before prescription

Prescribing a solution before understanding the problem is expensive. We spend significant time understanding your operational reality before a single line of code is written. This is how we avoid building the wrong thing.
03

AI is a multiplier, not a product

We don't sell AI. We use it — strategically — where it creates 10x leverage. The wrong AI in the wrong workflow creates noise. The right AI in the right place creates compounding advantage.
04

Simplicity scales. Complexity breaks.

Every system we design is built to be understood, operated, and expanded by your team. We don't create dependencies. We create capabilities. Simple, elegant systems outlast clever, complex ones.
05

Speed without shortcuts

We operate at startup speed without sacrificing structural integrity. Fast delivery means tight scoping, clear priorities, and relentless focus — not cutting corners on architecture or quality.
06

We own the outcome

We don't hand off a deliverable and disappear. We stay accountable to results. If the system isn't working, neither are we. This is what separates a real partner from a vendor.

What types of companies does ZOVAC work with?
+
We work best with operations-heavy businesses — typically SMEs, scale-ups, and funded startups that have hit operational ceilings. If your company is growing but your internal systems aren't keeping up, that's precisely the gap we close. Industry-agnostic: we've worked in services, logistics, and internal operations contexts.
How is this different from hiring a dev agency or a consultant?
+
A consultant diagnoses and leaves. A dev agency builds what you tell them. ZOVAC does both — we diagnose your operational problem ourselves, design the right system architecture, build it, integrate AI where it creates real leverage, and measure outcomes. You don't need to coordinate three vendors. You work with one team that owns the result end to end.
What does an engagement typically look like?
+
Engagements start with a diagnostic call where we map your current operational landscape and identify the highest-leverage bottlenecks. From there we scope a project — typically a 4–8 week build cycle — or a retainer for ongoing operational intelligence. Pricing ranges from project-based ($10k–$50k) to monthly retainers ($2k–$10k), depending on scope.
Do you use AI in everything you build?
+
No — and that's intentional. AI is a tool, not a requirement. We use AI where it creates genuine leverage: predictive analytics, intelligent automation, decision support, pattern recognition in large datasets. Where simpler, more reliable engineering solves the problem better, we use that. We don't add AI for the sake of it.
How long does a typical project take?
+
Our flagship management portal case study deployed in 6 weeks. Most projects run 4–10 weeks depending on complexity. We operate at startup speed — tight scoping, clear priorities, rapid iterations — without sacrificing structural quality. You'll see working software in week 2, not week 8.
What happens after the project is delivered?
+
We offer ongoing retainer arrangements for companies that want continuous operational intelligence — regular system audits, performance tracking, new feature development, and strategic guidance as your business scales. We don't disappear after launch.
Is our data and information kept confidential?
+
Absolutely. We operate under NDA by default on all engagements. Client identity and operational data are protected. We only share case studies with explicit written permission, and even then we anonymize where preferred. You can review our full Privacy Policy here.
