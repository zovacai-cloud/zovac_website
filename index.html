What We Stand For

Our Beliefs

Not a mission statement. The operating principles that shape every decision, every system, and every engagement we take on.

01

Outcomes over outputs

We don't measure success in code shipped or slides delivered. We measure it in efficiency gained, revenue unlocked, and decisions accelerated. If it doesn't move the needle, we didn't do our job.
02

Diagnosis before prescription

Prescribing a solution before understanding the problem is expensive. We spend real time mapping your operational reality before a single line of code is written — this is how we avoid building the wrong thing, fast.
03

AI is a multiplier, not a product

We don't sell AI. We deploy it where it creates compounding leverage: predictive analytics, intelligent automation, decision support. The wrong AI in the wrong workflow creates noise. The right AI, properly integrated, changes everything.
04

Simplicity scales. Complexity breaks.

Every system we build is designed to be understood, operated, and expanded by your team without us in the room. We don't create dependencies. We create capability. Simple, elegant architecture outlasts clever, fragile complexity every time.
05

Speed without shortcuts

We operate at startup speed without sacrificing structural integrity. Fast delivery means tight scoping, clear priorities, and focused execution — not cutting corners on architecture that has to hold your business for the next three years.
06

We own the outcome

We don't hand off a deliverable and disappear. We stay accountable to the result. If the system isn't creating the impact we promised, neither are we done. This is what separates a real operational partner from a vendor.

What types of businesses does ZOVAC work with?
+
ZOVAC works best with operations-heavy businesses — SMEs, scale-ups, and funded startups that have hit operational ceilings. If your company is growing faster than your internal systems can handle, that's the gap we close. We're industry-agnostic: services, logistics, B2B, SaaS, and internal operations contexts all apply.
How is this different from hiring a dev agency or a consultant?
+
A consultant diagnoses and leaves. A dev agency builds what you tell them. ZOVAC does both — we diagnose your operational problem ourselves, design the right system architecture, build it, integrate AI where it creates real leverage, and measure outcomes. You don't coordinate three vendors or translate your vision twice. One team, full accountability, end to end.
What does an engagement look like in practice?
+
Every engagement starts with a free 30-minute diagnostic call where we map your operational landscape and identify the highest-leverage bottlenecks. From there we scope a project — typically 4–8 weeks — or a retainer for ongoing operational intelligence. Project-based work ranges from $10k–$50k; monthly retainers from $2k–$10k depending on scope. You'll see working software by week 2.
Do you use AI in everything you build?
+
No — and that's intentional. AI is a tool, not a requirement. We use it where it creates genuine 10x leverage: predictive analytics, intelligent automation, decision support, pattern recognition across large datasets. Where simpler, more reliable engineering solves the problem better, we use that. We don't add AI to impress — we add it when the ROI is clear.
How quickly can ZOVAC deploy a working system?
+
Our flagship case study went from diagnosis to deployment in 6 weeks. Most projects run 4–10 weeks depending on complexity. We operate at startup speed — tight scoping, clear priorities, rapid iterations. You'll see functional software in week 2, not a wireframe review in week 6.
What happens after the project ships?
+
We offer ongoing retainer arrangements for companies that want continuous operational intelligence — regular system audits, performance tracking, new feature cycles, and strategic guidance as your business scales. We don't disappear after launch, and we don't charge for support calls that should have been avoided by building it right the first time.
Is our data and business information kept confidential?
+
Absolutely. We operate under NDA by default on all engagements. Client identity and operational data are fully protected. We only share case studies with explicit written permission, and anonymise where preferred. You can review our full Privacy Policy here.
